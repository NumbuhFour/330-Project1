About

This is a small game written for 330-Rich Media Web App Dev

The concept is a platformer jumping from gravitational bodies.
The spinning dotted lines mark the influence range of each planet. Moving within them will cause you to be affected by the planet's gravity.

The goal (to be implemented) will be to collect the gold on each of the planets while avoiding dangers and maintaining fuel.
